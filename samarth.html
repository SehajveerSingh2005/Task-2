BIO

Samarth was born in Indore, India to parents Mukesh Jurel.[4] His family consists of his parents and older brother Teerth Jurel. He studied at The Emerald Heights International School, graduated from DAVV University earning a bachelor's degree in Business Administration and took an acting course from Mumbai Film School. Growing up, he followed the footsteps of his father by pursuing a career in cricket in 2018 and went on to win two awards in the category of "Player of the Match" and "Best Batter" in 2022.[5]
